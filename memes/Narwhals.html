Narwhals: The Adorable Pranksters of the Sea

Narwhals are some of the cutest and most playful creatures you'll find in the ocean. They love nothing more than pranking their friends and having a good time.

Did you know that narwhals have a long, spiral tusk that's actually a tooth? It can grow up to 10 feet long and is used for everything from catching fish to jousting with other narwhals.
