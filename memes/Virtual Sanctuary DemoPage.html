About Us

Welcome to Virtual Sanctuary, the ultimate VRChat club for all virtual reality enthusiasts!

Our mission is to provide a safe and welcoming environment for people to come together and explore the endless possibilities of VRChat. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the world of virtual reality, everyone is welcome here!

At Virtual Sanctuary, we host regular events and activities, including game tournaments, art shows, dance parties, and much more! We also have a friendly and active community where you can connect with like-minded individuals and make new friends from all around the world.
