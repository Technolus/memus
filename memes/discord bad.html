Why Discord is Crinnnnnnnge

Discord is a messaging app designed for gamers, but it has become popular with other communities as well. While it has some useful features, it has also become cringe-worthy for various reasons.

1. The Overuse of Emojis and GIFs

While emojis and GIFs can be a fun way to express emotions and reactions, they have become overused on Discord. It's not uncommon to see entire conversations filled with nothing but emojis and GIFs, which can be annoying and distracting.

2. The Toxicity of Some Communities

Some communities on Discord can be extremely toxic, with users hurling insults and engaging in cyberbullying. This kind of behavior is not only cringe-worthy, but it can also be harmful to individuals and the community as a whole.

3. The Poor Quality of Voice Chat

While Discord's voice chat feature can be useful for gaming and online meetings, the quality of the audio can be poor. Users may experience lag, stuttering, and dropped connections, making it difficult to communicate effectively.
