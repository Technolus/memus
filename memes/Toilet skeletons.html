How to Remove a Skeleton from Your Toilet

Step 1: Gather Materials

Before you begin, you will need the following:

  • Rubber gloves
  • Large trash bags
  • Plastic tongs or a grabber tool
  • Disinfectant spray
  • A hose or shower head with detachable nozzle

Step 2: Remove the Skeleton

Put on the rubber gloves and use the plastic tongs or grabber tool to carefully remove the skeleton from the toilet. Be sure to hold it firmly and keep it intact as much as possible.

Step 3: Clean and Disinfect

Place the skeleton in a large trash bag for disposal. Spray the entire toilet with disinfectant spray and let it sit for a few minutes. Use the hose or shower head to rinse the toilet thoroughly. Dispose of the gloves and any other materials used in the process.
