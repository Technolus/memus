Classic Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are a classic appetizer that everyone loves. They're creamy, tangy, and just the right amount of spicy. This recipe is easy to make and is the perfect thing to bring to a party or potluck.

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • Paprika, for garnish

Directions:

  1. Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise and remove the yolks. Place the yolks in a small bowl and mash with a fork.
  2. Add the mayonnaise, yellow mustard, salt, and black pepper to the mashed yolks and stir until well combined.
  3. Spoon the egg yolk mixture into the egg white halves.
  4. Sprinkle with paprika for garnish.
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Fried Egg Sandwich

A fried egg sandwich is the perfect breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It's quick to make and always hits the spot.

  • 2 slices bread
