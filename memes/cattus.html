Catnip Website

Meow! Welcome to my website, all about catnip!

What is catnip, you ask? It's a plant that makes us kitties go wild! Check out these catnip facts:

  • Catnip is a member of the mint family.
  • Most cats love catnip, but not all cats are affected by it.
  • The chemical in catnip that drives cats crazy is called nepetalactone.
